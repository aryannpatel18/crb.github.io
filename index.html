About Us
Care and Relief Foundation, Bhopal (CRB) which was primarily known as Covid Relief Brigade, is a registered NGO under Madhya Pradesh Societies Registration Act,1973. This initiative was started by our President Mr. Divender Singh Cheema along with his friends who joined him during the Second wave of Covid on 23rd April 2021 and since then we have been extending helping hands to those in need .
